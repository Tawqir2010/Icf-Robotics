About Our Team

Learn about our mission, goals, and what we do

Our Mission

ICF Robotics Team - where we Innovate, Compete, and stay Fierce - exists to inspire and educate young engineers in STEM fields through competitive robotics. We strive to build not just robots, but character, leadership skills, and a strong foundation in values such as teamwork, integrity, and excellence in technology and engineering.

Our Goals

  • Provide hands-on STEM education through robotics
  • Develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Foster teamwork and collaboration
  • Integrate Islamic values with modern technology
  • Compete at regional and state robotics competitions
  • Serve our community through outreach programs

About FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics competition for students in grades 7-12. Teams design, build, and program robots to compete in an alliance format against other teams. The program teaches students about science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) through exciting, mentor-based programs that build not only science and technology skills, but also self-confidence, leadership, and life skills.

Design & Build

Teams design and build robots using a reusable platform, powered by Android technology.

Program

Students program their robots using Java-based programming or visual block programming.

Compete

Teams compete in exciting alliance competitions at tournaments throughout the season.
